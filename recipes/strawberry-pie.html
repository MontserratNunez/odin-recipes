Strawberry Pie

A delicious strawberry pie

Strawberry pies, with their ruby-red allure, are a celebration of summer’s bounty. Imagine a flaky, buttery crust cradling a luscious filling of ripe strawberries. Each berry bursts with sweetness, releasing its fragrant juices as it bakes. Whether adorned with a lattice top or a simple crumble, strawberry pies evoke memories of picnics, sun-kissed orchards, and lazy afternoons.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Arrange 1/2 of strawberries in baked pastry shell.
  2. Add sugar to the strawberries in the saucepan and place over medium heat.
  3. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a small bowl and gradually stir it to the strawberries.
  4. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell.
  5. Whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form.
  6. Enjoy!
