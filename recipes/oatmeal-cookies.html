Oatmeal Cookies

Recipe for oatmeal cookies

These cookies are a comforting blend of chewiness and crunch, perfect for a cozy afternoon snack or accompanying a warm cup of tea. The star ingredient, rolled oats, lends a hearty texture and a subtle nutty flavor. Whether studded with plump raisins, rich chocolate chips, or fragrant spices like cinnamon and nutmeg, oatmeal cookies evoke feelings of home and nostalgia.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats

Steps

  1. Mix the flour. cinnamon, baking soda and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix the butter and sugar in a large bowl until creamy, then add the eggs and mix until well combined. Then add the oats.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  4. Make little balls with the dough and place then in a tray.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until light golden brown.
