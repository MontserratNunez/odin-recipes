Lasagna

A fantastitc lasagna

Lasagna, a classic Italian dish, is a symphony of layers and flavors. Picture tender sheets of pasta, generously slathered with rich marinara sauce, interspersed with creamy béchamel and a medley of cheeses—mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan. Each forkful reveals a harmonious blend of savory ground meat, sautéed onions, and garlic.

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the meat, onion and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water.
  4. Combine ricotta cheese with egg and salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  6. In a baking dish, create some layers spreading some meat and then cheese.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
